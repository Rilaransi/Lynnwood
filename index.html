Welcome to Lynnwood Medical Clinic

Located in the heart of the Millcan/Ogden/Lynnwood neighbourhood in SE Calgary, we are a family medicine clinic dedicated to providing comprehensive, compassionate care for you and your loved ones.

At Lynnwood Medical Clinic, your health and well-being are our top priority. We offer a range of primary care services in a friendly, comfortable environment, ensuring personalized care for every patient.

Our Services

At Lynnwood Medical Clinic we are dedicated to delivering compassionate, high-quality, evidence based medical care.

Family Medicine

Family medicine is a comprehensive healthcare specialty focused on providing holistic care for individuals of all ages.

Rheumatology

Rheumatology specializes in diagnosing and treating autoimmune and inflammatory conditions affecting the joints, muscles, and bones to improve mobility and quality of life.

Mosaic Primary Care Network Partner

Primary Care Networks (PCNs) are groups of general practices that work together to expand services, improve access, and deliver coordinated care to meet local health needs.

Independent Medical Examinations

Independent Medical Examinations (IMEs) are unbiased evaluations conducted by a neutral physician to assess an individual’s health or injury, typically for legal or insurance purposes.
