Our Services

At Lynnwood Medical Clinic we are dedicated to delivering compassionate, high-quality, evidence based medical care.

Physical Exams

We offer comprehensive physical exams to help maintain your health and detect potential issues early. Annual exams are recommended.

  • Blood Pressure Check
  • Height and Weight Measurement
  • Systemic Examinations

Diagnostic Tests

We provide requisitions for a range of diagnostic tests, including:

  • Blood Tests
  • STI Tests
  • Ultrasounds
  • X-rays
  • Mammograms
  • Bone Densitometry
  • Cardiac Tests

Prenatal Care

We offer quality prenatal care until your care is transferred to a maternity clinic, midwife, or specialist.

  • Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • Weight and Height Measurement
  • Fetal Heart Rate Check
  • Urine Analysis

Baby Check-ups

Regular check-ups for babies, including:

  • Head Circumference Measurement
  • Weight and Height Measurement
  • General Health Check

Pap Test Clinic

We offer Pap tests for cervical cancer screening.

IUD Clinic

We offer appointments for IUD consultation, insertion, or removal.

Endometrial Biopsy

We offer endometrial biopsy in clinic. Please speak to the receptionist to organize this.

Work Injuries & WCB Services

For work-related injuries, inform us during your visit and provide employer details for WCB documentation.

Immunizations

We offer the following AHS-insured and uninsured vaccines:

  • Seasonal Flu Vaccine
  • Pneumovax
  • Tetanus
  • Hepatitis A & B
  • Gardasil
  • Shingrix

Driver's Medical

This is an uninsured service and is billed privately.

Out-of-Province Patients

We accept out-of-province patients. Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID and health card. Quebec residents must pay upfront for services.
